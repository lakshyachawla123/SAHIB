RULES

Information on Referee Responsibility and Cards
🟥 Red Card
▪️Suspension from only current playing match.
▪️Two consecutive red cards in two matches will lead to suspension from the third match.
🟨 Yellow Card
▪️Suspension for 3 minutes in current playing match.
▪️Two yellow cards in a same match equals to one red card.
Responsibilities of Referee
▪️ Argument - Incase, where a player argues with a referee against a decision or manipulates him. A referee can assess the situation and decide to give a decision based on the following three references.
1. When severity of argument is LOW = Warning
2. When severity of argument is MEDIUM = Yellow Card 🟨
3. When severity of argument is HIGH = Red Card 🟥
▪️ Foul - Incase, when a foul occurs. A referee can assess the situation and decide to give a decision based on the following three references.
1. When severity of foul is LOW = Warning
2. When severity of foul is MEDIUM = Yellow Card 🟨
3. When severity of foul is HIGH = Red Card 🟥
Note:
Severity of an argument/foul is subjective opinion of the referee assigned and varies from person to person.
